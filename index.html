3. Information We Collect

3.1 Data You Provide

When you use TubeMinder, you may enter the following information, which is stored locally on your device only:

  • Reminder Data: Video titles, YouTube URLs, reminder messages, scheduled date and time, category labels, and repeat preferences that you create within the App.
  • Playlist Data: YouTube playlist URLs and associated video information when you import a playlist.
  • Watch History: Records of reminders you mark as watched, stored locally for your personal tracking.
  • App Preferences: Your notification sound selection, vibration preference, and other settings.

3.2 Data Collected by Third-Party Services

TubeMinder uses Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) to display advertisements. AdMob automatically collects and processes certain data, including:

  • Device & Account Identifiers: Android advertising ID (ad ID), app set ID, and identifiers tied to signed-in accounts on the device.
  • IP Address: Used to estimate the general location of the device for ad targeting.
  • User Product Interactions: App launch, taps, video views, and other interaction information related to ads.
  • Diagnostic Information: Performance data such as app launch time, hang rate, and energy usage.

This data is collected for the purposes of advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention. All data collected by the Google Mobile Ads SDK is encrypted in transit using the Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol.

The Android advertising ID is optional — users can reset or delete their advertising ID through device settings (Settings > Google > Ads), and users can opt out of personalized ads.

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Partner Policy. We do not have access to or control over this data.

3.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

TubeMinder itself does NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Account or login credentials
  • Location data (GPS, network-based, or approximate)
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Photos, videos, or files from your device
  • Browsing history or search history outside the App
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data
  • Analytics, diagnostics, or crash report data

4. How We Use Information

All data stored by TubeMinder is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Scheduling Reminders: To set alarms and send you notifications at your chosen date and time so you don't miss a video.
  • Displaying Content: To show your saved reminders, watch history, playlists, and statistics within the App interface.
  • Loading Thumbnails: To fetch and display YouTube video thumbnail images from YouTube's public servers when displaying your reminders.
  • Backup & Restore: To allow you to export your reminders to a local file and restore them when needed. This process is entirely local and user-initiated.
  • Notification Customization: To apply your preferred notification sound and vibration settings when sending reminders.

5. Internet Usage

TubeMinder requires the INTERNET permission for the following purposes:

  • YouTube Thumbnail Images: Fetching video thumbnail images from YouTube's public image servers (i.ytimg.com) to display alongside your reminders.
  • Playlist Information: Fetching publicly available playlist data from YouTube when you import a playlist.
  • Advertisements: Loading and displaying ads through Google AdMob. Ad content is served from Google's ad network servers. This includes transmitting device identifiers, IP address, user interaction data, and diagnostic information to Google for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes.

Your personal reminder data is never transmitted over the internet. The internet connection is used for fetching publicly available YouTube content and for serving advertisements.

6. Data Storage & Security

6.1 Local Storage

All reminder data, watch history, playlist information, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using:

  • Room Database (SQLite): For structured reminder and watch history data.
  • SharedPreferences: For app settings such as notification sound and vibration preferences.

This data resides in the App's private storage directory, which is protected by the Android operating system and is not accessible to other applications.

6.2 Security Measures

  • All data is stored in the app's sandboxed private storage, protected by Android's built-in security model.
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers — we do not operate any servers.
  • The App uses Google AdMob for advertisements, which operates under Google's own security standards.
  • The release build uses R8 code shrinking and obfuscation for additional protection.

7. Data Sharing with Third Parties

7.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

TubeMinder uses Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) to display advertisements. Through AdMob, Google automatically collects and shares the following data for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes:

  • Device and account identifiers (Android advertising ID, app set ID)
  • IP address
  • User product interactions (app launch, taps, video views)
  • Diagnostic information (performance data)

All data collected by AdMob is encrypted in transit using TLS. The Android advertising ID is optional and can be reset or deleted by the user through device settings.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.

7.2 What We Do NOT Share

We do not:

  • Sell, rent, or trade any user data to any third party.
  • Use any third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics).
  • Integrate any social media tracking or login SDKs.
  • Send any of your reminder data, watch history, or personal information to any server.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

8.1 Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as:

  • The App is installed on your device, OR
  • You choose to keep the data within the App.

8.2 Deletion

You have complete control over your data. You can delete your data at any time through the following methods:

  • Within the App: Delete individual reminders by swiping or using the delete option. Clear watch history through the app interface.
  • Clear App Data: Go to your device's Settings > Apps > TubeMinder > Storage > Clear Data. This removes all app data.
  • Uninstall the App: Uninstalling TubeMinder automatically removes all associated data from your device.

Since no data is stored on external servers, deletion from your device means the data is permanently and irreversibly removed.

9. App Permissions

TubeMinder requests the following Android permissions, each for a specific and necessary purpose:

Permission Purpose
INTERNET Fetch YouTube video thumbnails, playlist metadata, and load advertisements via Google AdMob.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Send reminder notifications at your scheduled times (Android 13+).
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM Schedule precise alarms so reminders fire at the exact time you set.
USE_EXACT_ALARM Ensures alarms are delivered precisely, required on Android 12+.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Reschedule your reminders after a device restart so no reminders are lost.
WAKE_LOCK Briefly wake the device to process and deliver reminder notifications.
REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS Request exemption from battery optimization to ensure reminders are delivered reliably.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE Run a short-lived foreground service to reliably deliver notifications.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_REMINDERS Categorizes the foreground service as a reminder service (Android 14+).
No sensitive permissions are requested. TubeMinder does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, phone, SMS, storage (beyond its own private directory), or any other sensitive device features.

10. Your Rights & Controls

As a user of TubeMinder, you have the following rights over your data:

  • Right to Access: All your data is visible and accessible within the App at all times — reminders, watch history, and settings.
  • Right to Modify: You can edit any reminder, change categories, update times, and modify all settings at any time.
  • Right to Delete: You can delete any individual reminder, clear your watch history, or remove all data by clearing app data or uninstalling.
  • Right to Export: You can export all your reminders to a backup file using the Backup feature, giving you a portable copy of your data.
  • Right to Restrict Permissions: You can revoke any permission at any time through your device's Settings > Apps > TubeMinder > Permissions. The App will continue to function with reduced capabilities.

Since all data is stored locally and we have no access to it, you exercise these rights directly through the App and your device settings — no request to us is needed.
